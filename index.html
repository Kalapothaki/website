NTUA ARCH 4342 Computational Methods in Creative Arts and Sciences

Lida Kalapothaki

I am a multidisciplinary creative professional with a background in Marketing and Advertising from the University of the Arts London, and Interior Design from Central Saint Martins. I lived and worked in London for eight years, gaining hands-on experience in design and visual communication. After returning to Greece, I worked in architectural firms as an interior designer, bringing a refined aesthetic and international perspective to a range of residential and commercial projects. Currently based in Aspra Spitia, Viotia, I continue to be inspired by the arts and the built environment. As a lifelong admirer of design, art, and architecture, I’ve recently embarked on a new journey to fulfill my dream of becoming an architect. In pursuit of this vision, through this class Computational Approaches in Creative Arts and Sciences—a field that merges technology with artistic expression by integrating computational tools, I aim to enhance my creative process and bring innovative, future-forward ideas into the spaces I design. Beyond my professional life, I’m a proud mother of two children, who inspire me every day to keep growing, learning, and creating.
